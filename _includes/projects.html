Projects and Research Experience

  Change-point Detection
  • As thesis topic, researching on methods including Bayesian frameworks and neural networks for multivariate time series change and anomaly detection. Data applications include periodic return of stocks, genetic profiles and prognostics measurements. Contributed to the R package ecp.
  Time Series
  • Predictive modeling of transactions data to improve financial planning capabilities for low-wage workers at Science for Social Good initiative.
  • Clustering and classification of multivariate time series using dynamic time warping techniques, applied to human activity recognition through smartphone motion sensor data.
  • ORIE 6070 paper review: Estimation of the structural mean of curves.
  Neural Networks
  • Developed neural network models for feature extraction and missing modality imputation on multimodal sensor data at Institute of Infocomm Research, S&S Program, 2013 - 2014.
  Data Visualization
  • Built R Shiny apps to facilitate data exploration and interpretation during work/internship stints.
  Biology
